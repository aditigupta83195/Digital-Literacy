For this task, I used Canva’s AI-powered design tools to create an infographic aimed at educating college students about digital literacy. My design focuses on three key areas: safe internet practices (such as identifying phishing attempts and creating strong passwords), essential digital tools (including collaboration platforms and cloud storage), and building a professional online presence (like optimizing profiles on platforms such as LinkedIn).

One of the main challenges I faced during this project was striking the right balance between textual information and visual elements. Initially, I attempted to include detailed explanations for each concept, thinking it would make the infographic more informative. However, this approach made the design look overcrowded and less engaging. I realized that an effective infographic should communicate ideas quickly and clearly. As a result, I revised my content several times, simplifying the text and focusing only on the most essential points.

With the help of Canva AI’s layout suggestions, I explored different ways to improve the visual appeal and readability of my design. I incorporated icons, consistent color schemes, and clear sectioning to create a strong visual hierarchy. These elements helped guide the viewer’s attention and made the information easier to understand at a glance. I also paid attention to spacing and alignment to ensure the infographic looked clean and professional.

Overall, this experience was both engaging and educational. It taught me that effective digital communication is a balance between content and presentation. Clear visuals, concise text, and thoughtful design choices play a crucial role in making information accessible and impactful.
